Frequently Asked Questions

1. How to book an appointment?

Press Book Appointment Button given in your consultant’s profile page. Choose the service you want and then it will show the available slots. Choose the slot you want and make payment. You will receive email confirmation from bookconsult.in. If not found in inbox, please check spam and promotions. If you still didn’t receive, please contact us by whatsapp text with the details. We will check the status and inform you.

2. Will the Payments be secure?

Payment part is fully taken care by Razorpay Payment Gateway and Stripe Payment Gateway. These are trustworthy gateways. Your payment data is processed in their websites. In case of any issues in the payment, you can contact their 24/7 customer care also. If you are worried about giving your card details in gateways, please use NetBanking via Razorpay Payment Gateway, you will not give any bank related details in the website of the Gateway. You will just choose the bank under NetBanking and go to your bank website only. Please check whether your bank website is secure with “https” in the URL, check for the spelling of your bank in the address bar and see whether it looks the same way it was before and make the payment. Moreover ours is a secure website with “https”. You can click on the lock symbol of the address bar and check the full details of our SSL Certificate which proves that the data you give in this site is encrypted and secure. For reference, you may take screenshots of the payment.

3. How to cancel/reschedule the session?

Sorry, it is not possible for clients to cancel or reschedule the session. Please read our Cancellation & Refund Policy before you make the payment. No refund will be given if you do not attend the session.

4. Payment is taken but appointment was not booked. What to be done?

Do not worry! Just share details of your payment such as which payment gateway whether Stripe or Razorpay, amount deducted and time of the payment, we will get back with the status.
